AI Greetings – User Guide

Welcome to the AI Greetings application! This guide will help you quickly get started, explore all features, and troubleshoot common issues. If you have further questions, please contact our support team at support (at) mAIstermind.com.

1. Getting Started

  1. Open the Application: Visit https://greetings.maistermind.com. You will land on the login or registration page.
  2. Register a New Account:
    • Click "Register", fill in your email and create a password.
    • Accept the Privacy Policy & Terms, then click "Sign Up".
    • Tip: Use a strong, unique password for your account.
  3. Verify Your Email (if required):
    • Check your inbox for a confirmation email and click the verification link (if your plan requires this step).
  4. Log In: Enter your email and password, then click "Log In" to access the dashboard.

2. Creating a New Greeting Card

  1. On your dashboard, click the "New Greeting" button or the plus icon (+).
  2. Fill out the card details form:
    • Choose the occasion (Birthday, Thank You, Holiday, etc.).
    • Enter the recipient's name (optional).
    • Add any personalized message or additional details.
  3. (Optional) Click "Customize Image" to request a specific card artwork or pick a style from the gallery.
  4. Click "Generate". The AI will craft a unique message and artwork for your greeting card.
  5. Review your generated card and make edits if needed.
  6. Click "Save" to keep your card or "Send" to share it.

3. Sending a Greeting Card

  • In your card list, locate the card you wish to send.
  • Click the paper airplane or "Send" button next to the card.
  • Choose to send via:
    • Email: Enter the recipient’s email address and (optional) a custom message.
    • Download: Save the card as an image or PDF to send manually.
    • Shareable Link: Copy a private unique link to your card and share it via chat, SMS, or social media.

4. Editing or Deleting Cards

  1. Click the ellipsis menu (…) or right-click on the card.
  2. Select "Edit" to update your message or image, then Save.
  3. Select "Delete" to permanently remove your card.
  4. Warning: Deleting a card is irreversible. Download or save your card before deleting if you may need it later.

5. Account Settings

  1. Click on your profile icon or Settings in the main menu.
  2. You can update:
    • Your display name and email address.
    • Your password (requires current password for security).
    • Notification preferences (enable/disable email updates).
    • Subscription or billing information (under Subscription or Billing tab).
  3. To log out, click "Log Out" at the bottom of the menu.

6. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Question Answer
How many cards can I generate per month? Your quota depends on your subscription plan. To check your limit, visit Settings > Subscription.
Why did my generated card fail? The card generator may fail due to a network/server error or if your monthly quota has been reached. Try again in a few minutes or upgrade your plan.
Can I edit my card design after generating? Yes! Click "Edit" on any card to tweak the message and style before sending.
How do I reset my password? On the login page, click "Forgot Password?" and follow the instructions sent to your email.
Is my data private? All your card messages and recipient info are secured and never shared. See our Privacy Policy for details.

7. Shortcuts & Tips

  • Ctrl + N: Create a new greeting card quickly.
  • Ctrl + S: Save the current card.
  • /?: Access help docs from any page.
  • Pro Tip: You can paste a whole story or prompt into the message box—the AI can handle long text!

8. Troubleshooting

  • Forgot your password?
    • Go to the login page and click "Forgot Password?" Follow the emailed reset instructions.
  • Card is not generating or app is slow?
    • Check your internet connection.
    • If you have a quota, ensure you have not exceeded it.
    • Try logging out and logging back in, or refreshing the page.
  • Still need help?
    • Contact support (at) mAIstermind.com with any issues, including screenshots if possible.
